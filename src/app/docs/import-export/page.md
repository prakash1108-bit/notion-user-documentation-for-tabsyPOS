# **Data**

## **Import & Export**

The **Data Import & Export** feature allows you to **back up, restore, or migrate** your store data quickly and securely — all without requiring cloud access or an active internet connection.

You can easily import or export the following types of data:

* **Products:** Complete product catalogue with names and prices.
* **Customers:** Full customer database, including contact details.
* **Sales:** Comprehensive transaction history for all completed sales.
* **Sales Items:** Item-level details for each transaction.
* **Sales Payments:** Payment information linked to each sale.
* **Receipt Settings:** Customised receipt header and footer templates.
* **Other Settings:** Store preferences such as **language**, **currency**, and **AI model configuration**.
For enhanced data security, you can also **back up your store data to Google Drive** directly from this section — ensuring your information is safely stored and easily restorable whenever needed.
